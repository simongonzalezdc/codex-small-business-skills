## Aggregator Pitch

Use this description for list submissions:

```text
Apache-2.0 Codex port of Anthropic's Small Business skills: 31 owner-operator workflows for cash flow, invoice follow-up, CRM cleanup, customer support, lead triage, content strategy, hiring, and weekly business rhythm, with approval gates before risky business actions.
```

## Safety Notes

- Do not imply Anthropic endorses this port.
- Keep attribution to the upstream Apache-2.0 source visible.
- Avoid submitting to directories whose rules require stars, official listing, or
  non-agent/manual-only submission before those conditions are true.
- Prefer pull requests over issues when the directory has a clear README format.
